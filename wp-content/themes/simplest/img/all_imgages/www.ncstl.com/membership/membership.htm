Membership

Annual membership is now just $20 (single) or $25 (family), with no frills attached (you get a membership card, quarterly newsletter, access to the private NCPS eGroup, access to a members section of the website, discounts on some merchandise and that's it for now).

The first payment is actually an application fee.  Thereafter, dues are payable annually on May 1.
