Employee Database

For the past several years, NCPS has slowly begun building an database of former NC&StL Employees. The project is massive and will be on-going for many years to come.  Recently 8,000 listings were uploaded to the NCPS Member Section.  Members are now able to search these listings by name, and will soon be able to add listings of their own. 

This feature will eventually be moved to the public website, whenever testing is complete.  Please let me know what you think... send email
