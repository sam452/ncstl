NC&StL Diesel Roster

Regarding the number in brackets next to a road number (e.g. 10[2] for S2 builders number 74438). This is not a footnote but refers
to the 2nd NC&StL Diesel loco to have this road number. If you look at the "notes" column for the VO660 you will see 10[1] along
with other info. Apparently, when delivered, the VO660 was assigned road number 10. But when the S2 arrived the VO660 was
renumbered to 69 and 10 was used for an S2. If you search through the roster you should (I hope) find a [1] for every [2] and visa-versa.

Information on these 132 locomotives was obtained from two books. (1) Diesel Locomotive Roster: Louisville & Nashville Railroad (1929-1983) by Robert Sells and (2) Louisville and Nashville Diesel Locomotives by Charles Castner, Ron Flanary & Lee Gordon. Both of these books list the heritage of all L&N diesel locomotives in various foot notes. By comparing the two sources I reordered the info using the NC&StL road number as the primary sort (rather than the L&N road number). I did not discover any disagreement between the two books so I guess the roster is reasonably accurate (unless I transcribed something wrong).
