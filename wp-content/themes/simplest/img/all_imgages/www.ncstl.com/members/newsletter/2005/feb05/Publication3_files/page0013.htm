Look at these pictures from the perspective of what it might have looked like in the early Twenties, fresh from shopping at Shops if anyone had been around with a camera with color film in it…yes, I know that there is a boatload of inaccuracies with this model but this one of those dummy AHM units I got from that guy here in Austin that deals in junk trains so my true out of pocket expenses here, including the details and paint bring this one in under ten bucks….

 

(Ed. Note:  Homemade NC&StL Safety First decals duplicate an authentic safety drive by the Railway during that time.  This project is a relatively straight forward use of a ready-made Pacific converted to a very convincing early Class K-1.  Nice job, isn’t it? — Tom Knowles)
