A very special part of this fine train was the steam locomotive that was streamlined to pull it.  No. 535, built by Baldwin in 1931, a Pacific type 4-6-2 (C/N 39450).  Mr. C. M. Darden, Supt. of Machinery for the NC&StL chose this fine engine to rebuild and streamline for the City of Memphis.  Not long after she arrived on her home rails she was christened the “Marie” in honor of Marie Flinn Harrison, the five year old daughter of the Rev.
