In the early 1900’s, Cowan added a cement company and several more retail businesses to its industrial base. The University of the South at Sewanee continued to grow, and helped make Cowan an important connection for the "Mountain Goat" (the term given the NC’s Tracy City Branch) running from Cowan to up to Sewanee, Tracy City and Coalmont.

 

Cowan was finally incorporated in 1922 and chartered with the State of Tennessee. Shortly afterwards Cowan garnered a new manufacturing industry, a new public school,
